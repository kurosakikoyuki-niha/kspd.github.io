Community Safety Bureau

Overview

The Public Security Bureau is responsible for maintaining peace, public order, and emergency response operations throughout the Valkyrie region. As the front-line branch of K.S.P.D, its officers are trained in rapid deployment, tactical coordination, and crisis suppression.

Responsibilities

  • Urban patrol and frontline security operations
  • Quick response to emergent criminal activity
  • High-risk intervention capability
  • Coordination with tactical & forensic units

Training & Qualifications

Officers selected for the PSB receive specialized training in high-intensity intervention, public defense doctrine, and academy-licensed tactical conduct.
